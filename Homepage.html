What are the minimum requirements?
  • Latest version of wordpress or the one previous
  • PHP 5.3.3+ compiled with the cURL extension
  • (we do not support safe mode)
  • cURL 7.16.2+ compiled with OpenSSL and zlib
  • MySQL 5.0+
  • Apache 2+ or Ngnix 1.4+

It will likely work with other web servers that supports PHP & MySQL, but we do not officially support those.
